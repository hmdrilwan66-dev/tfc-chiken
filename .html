About us

TFC nation

tender fride Chiken is a multi-cuisine fine-dining restaurant in Salem, Tamil Nadu, known for its rich blend of Indian, Thai, Chinese, and Continental flavors. Established by the founders of the renowned Rasikas Restaurant, it brings two decades of culinary heritage into a contemporary, globally inspired menu and setting.

the restaurant’s concept revolves around fusion cuisine, combining authentic Asian and Indian techniques with international presentation. Signature dishes include Phuket Fish, Thai Basil Chicken, Butter Garlic Prawns, and Chettinad Mutton Curry, alongside classic biryanis, tandoori platters, and an extensive selection of mocktail.
